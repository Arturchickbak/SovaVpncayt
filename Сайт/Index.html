Как загрузить конфигурацию OpenVPN на iPhone

Step 1: Download OpenVPN Connect

Go to the App Store and search for "OpenVPN Connect." Install the app on your device.

Step 2: Obtain Configuration Files

Download the OpenVPN config file (.ovpn) from your provider.

Step 3: Import the Configuration

Open the OpenVPN Connect app and import the .ovpn file using the "Import" option.

Step 4: Connect to VPN

After importing, tap on "Connect" and allow permissions. Your VPN should now be active.
